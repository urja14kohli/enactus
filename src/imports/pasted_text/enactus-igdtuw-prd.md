# WEBSITE PRD — ENACTUS IGDTUW

## Project Overview

Design and develop a premium, modern, highly professional website for Enactus IGDTUW.

The website must feel like a global movement, not a college society.

Primary inspiration should come from the official Enactus Global website, particularly its visual language, geometric transitions, large typography, editorial storytelling style, angular dividers, and premium nonprofit aesthetic.

The website should communicate:

* Leadership
* Innovation
* Entrepreneurship
* Social Impact
* Community
* Global Alignment

The overall perception should be:

"Enactus IGDTUW operates with the professionalism of a global organization while retaining the energy and creativity of student leadership."

---

# BRAND POSITIONING

Enactus IGDTUW is not merely a student club.

It is a community of student leaders who create sustainable impact through entrepreneurial action.

The website should emphasize:

People first.
Impact second.
Ventures third.

Visitors should remember the people, stories, and mission before they remember individual projects.

---

## Visual Direction

Premium.

# GLOBAL DESIGN LANGUAGE

Use Enactus-inspired geometric branding throughout the website.

No straight section breaks.

Every section transition should use:

* Diagonal cuts
* Angular separators
* Geometric wedges
* Floating yellow brand shapes

Yellow geometric elements should appear repeatedly as a recognizable visual motif.

Examples:

Corner triangles

Angular dividers

Floating geometric accents

Diagonal section entries and exits

The entire page should feel dynamic and in motion.
